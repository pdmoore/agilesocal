AgileSoCal Monthly Meeting - Wednesday, May 18, 5:00 PM

Deadlines with Dave Moore

When: Wednesday, May 18th
Networking and food, 5:00 PM.
Activities start, 5:30 PM.

IMPORTANT NOTE - We are meeting in Pasadena this month!

***** Always free, but YOU MUST RSVP to agilesocal@gmail.com *****

Deadline pressure is usually a taboo subject. Deadlines often lead to poor quality, overworked employees, and low team morale. As a consultant Dave Moore has been in the trenches at a lot of organizations big and small. This talk is a combination of his observations and strategies he has implemented around getting in front of deadline pressure. As an industry this topic is becoming increasingly important as society's dependence on working software grows stronger. You can expect to leave this session with ideas on things you can change at your workplace to increase effective communication with stakeholders and lessen the burden of deadline pressure.

Your Presenter:

As Director of Software Services, Dave Moore serves as the lead project manager for every client at 8th Light's Los Angeles office. He holds extensive meetings with clients, and develops plans to translate their ideas into concrete, deliverable software that accomplishes their goals. He bootstraps each new project, and helps coordinate and prioritize the team's workflow.
